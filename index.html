THE FLAGSHIP GAME

A WORLD THAT NEVER RESETS

Most arcade games forget you the moment you walk away. We're building one that never forgets anything. One shared world — towns, fields, and the dark places under them — that the whole room plays through together, twenty-five chapters a season. When a chapter is written, it's written for everyone, forever.

① ONE RUN A DAY
One free run per person. That's the whole price — and everyone is watching.
② A SHARED STORY
Every run moves the chapter meter. The number on the wall is the town's number.
③ THE LEDGER
Every deed recorded by name, forever. Glory and comedic death alike.
④ SEASONS
Every few months the world ends — climactically — and reforges. Glory carries forward.
The tone, for the record: the stakes are real, the world is ridiculous. Real heroism, talking skeletons, comedic deaths, a narrator who is not impressed with you.

THE REAL PLACE

DOORS OPEN 2027

The Quarter is a real arcade — cabinets, pinball, a floor full of the machines you remember. There is no building yet: we're starting from an empty lot, town approvals and foundation and framing and all. The location gets announced the day the paperwork clears.
